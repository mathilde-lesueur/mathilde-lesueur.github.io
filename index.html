Mathilde Lesueur

Postdoctoral Researcher in Economics · University of Bologna

I am a postdoctoral researcher in economics at the University of Bologna, working with Elisabetta De Cao on the ERC Consolidator Grant HARSH: Uncovering the roots of harsh parenting.

I hold a PhD in Economics from Paris-Dauphine University (PSL Research University). My research is in development, gender, and family economics.

I am a member of the Econometric Society, the European Economic Association (EEA), the AFEDEV Network, and I am affiliated with DIAL at Paris-Dauphine University.
